# Webmapping - Summary of the city of Zermatt

**Interactive mapping and urban mobility analysis for the city of Strasbourg**  
_L3PRO MPGE • ENSG-Géomatique • 2024-2025_

**Live demo**: https://bapti100.github.io/webmap.l3pro.baptiste.io/

---

## Summary

"Summary of the city of Zermatt" is a website that aims to highlight the city of Zermatt in a simple and effective way.

Some activities are proposed, photos and a web map showing the location of the city are available on this website.

---

## Features

The platform offers three web pages:

### 1. **The index**

- A summary of the city
- A registration form

### 2. **The galery**

- A grid of 3x3 images containing images of the station and the piste map

### 3. **The map**

- A map set up from the leaflet library
